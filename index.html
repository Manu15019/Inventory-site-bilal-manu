About This Project



This is our inventory tracker. We were asked to:


Make and automated online inventory that allows the school store to easily keep track of inventory and sales



This is relevant because:


This helps the student-run CRLS school store keeping track of stock, when to order, sales, etc.


This will allow the workers at the store to have all the information they need online, along with some helpful tools, ensuring an easy and seemless store experience.
