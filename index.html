About This Project



This is our inventory tracker. Originally given to us by our teacher Andrew Jang, graduate of Tufts University, this tracker was intended to help the CRLS school store automate their inventory.

  • TASK: Keep track of stock, Keep track of sales, and make it user friendly
  • RELEVANCE: Helps the school store(run by student), Informs consumers of stock amounts, and friendly for non CS workers
  • CHALLENGES: Lack of communicstion with the school store and project direction
  • REPLICATION: Figure how a noncoder can use your tool, YouTube & Chat-gpt, and most importantly, don't let the haters hate
  • This project encouraged us to tackle problems in a unique way
